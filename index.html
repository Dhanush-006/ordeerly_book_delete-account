Privacy Policy — Orderly

Overview

Orderly is an app to manage orders, products, customers and purchase bills. We built Orderly to make order management simple for small businesses, shop owners, and operators. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and your rights.

This policy covers:
  • What personal and non-personal data we collect
  • How and why we use that data
  • Your choices and rights
  • How to contact us

1. Data we collect

1.1 Personal information

When you sign up or use Orderly we may collect personal info such as:

  • Full name and display name
  • Phone number (for login / OTP) and email address
  • Business name, billing address and shipping address
  • Contact details for customers you manage
1.2 Usage & device data

We collect information about how you use the app and the device you use, including:

  • IP address, device model, OS, and browser info
  • App usage events and activity logs
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
1.3 Payment & billing data

Payment processing is handled by third-party gateways. We store transaction records (amount, date, order id) but we do not store full card details on our servers.

1.4 Optional data
  • Location data (only if you enable or use location features)
  • Profile pictures you upload
  • Notes, attachments, or files you attach to orders

2. How we use data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Process orders, invoices and payments
  • Send transactional messages such as receipts and order updates
  • Provide customer features like search, filters and reporting
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs and analyze usage

Example: When you create a bill, we store the bill details (items, prices, customer) so you can view and manage it later.

3. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS) for data in transit
  • Secure authentication (OTP / password / token)
  • Role-based access and least-privilege controls
  • Regular backups and vulnerability checks

Note: No system is 100% secure — if you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately.

4. Third-party services

Orderly uses trusted third parties for many services. Typical providers include:

  • Authentication & notifications (e.g., Firebase or similar)
  • Payment gateways (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal etc.)
  • Maps & geolocation providers (Google Maps, Mapbox)
  • Analytics & crash reporting (e.g., Google Analytics, Sentry)

Each provider has their own privacy rules — we only share the minimum data necessary and require providers to protect your data.

5. Your rights

You have choices about your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal retention rules).
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of marketing communications.
How to request changes

To request access, corrections or deletion, contact our support team (see Contact section). We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

6. Children's privacy

Orderly is not intended for children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that data.

7. Data retention

We retain account and transactional information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion — some records (e.g., tax invoices) may be retained as required by law.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we will notify users in the app and update the "Last updated" date above. Review this page periodically.
